The 8-Ingredient Science

100% Active Ingredients. No Fillers.

1. BeeswaxThe Physical Wall. Yellow beeswax provides the structural backbone of our shield, ensuring it stays firm and effective in our intense Foothills summers. It creates a dense, breathable barrier that no see-um mouthparts physically cannot penetrate, while sealing in moisture to help protect and repair weathered ranch skin.
2. LanolinThe Staying Power. We use pure New Zealand Merino wool grease. This is the natural oil that keeps a sheep’s skin protected from the wind and rain. On you, it ensures the shield stays put even when you’re working up a sweat in the heat, while providing deep moisture that softens up dry, cracked ranch hands.
3. Coconut OilThe Cloak. Rich in medium-chain fatty acids that mask your scent from searching sensors. Beyond the shield, it acts as a deep moisturizer that sinks into rough skin, providing a natural anti-microbial layer to keep old bites from getting angry.
4. Catnip OilThe Powerhouse. While university studies show that Catnip is 10x more effective than DEET, it’s also one of nature’s best skin soothers. Traditionally used for its calming properties, it helps take the edge off a stressful day in the field. Catnip also naturally helps ease the redness and irritation from old bites.
5. Cedarwood OilThe Anchor. A heavy base-note that binds the other oils to the wax. Cedarwood is also a primary defense against fleas and ticks.
6. Palmarosa OilThe Radar Jammer. Jams the navigation systems of biting midges (no see-ums). Rich in Geraniol, it serves as an extra layer of flea and tick defense.
7. Lavender OilThe Soother. Calms the inflammation of old bites and supports skin repair after a day in the field. Lavender is well-known for its aroma and calming effects.
8. Rosemary OilThe Preservative. Keeps the botanical blend of oils stable in the sun while also acting as a natural barrier against biting insects.
