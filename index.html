RancherShield wasn't made in a corporate lab. It was developed in the clay hills of Shingle Springs, California. As ranchers, we know that store-bought sprays don’t stand up to the 12-hour workdays and the aggressive biting midges of the foothills. We built a shield that works as hard as you do.

The Science of the Shield

1. Beeswax (The Physical Wall)
Midges don't just bite; they slice. Our raw beeswax creates a breathable, physical "wall" over your skin that their tiny mouthparts cannot penetrate.
2. Lanolin (The Grip)
We use pure USP Lanolin (wool grease). It is a waterproof adhesive that locks the balm to your skin so it stays on while you sweat or work in the sun.
3. Coconut Oil (The Invisibility Cloak)
Bugs find you by sensing body heat and lactic acid. Coconut oil contains fatty acids that act as a "cloak," masking your scent from their sensors.
4. Catnip Oil (The 10x Powerhouse)
Our active repellent. University studies show Catnip is 10x more effective than DEET. It triggers a pain response in the bug, forcing them to leave before they land.
5. Cedarwood Oil (The Anchor)
Cedarwood is a "fixative"—it binds the other scents to the wax so they last for hours. It is also your best natural defense against ticks.
6. Palmarosa Oil (The Radar Jammer)
Rich in geraniol, Palmarosa jams the sensory radar of no-see-ums, making you invisible to their navigation while adding a premium woody-rose scent.
7. Lavender & Rosemary (The Healing Team)
We added Lavender and Rosemary to settle the inflammation of old bites and help your skin repair itself while you’re in the field.
